# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

- **Be respectful:** Treat others with respect and consideration.
- **Be collaborative:** Focus on constructive feedback and helpful communication.
- **No harassment:** Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language will not be tolerated.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers at conduct@example.com.

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include removal from the project or other actions at the maintainers' discretion.
